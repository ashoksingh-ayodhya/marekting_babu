# Louzan Fashion — Digital Growth Roadmap
**April 2026 | 12-Month Horizon**

---

## The Goal
Close the gap between Louzan's physical brand strength (23 years, 25+ stores, ISO 9001) and its digital performance — and become the GCC's most recognised luxury abaya brand online.

---

## Top of Funnel — Build Awareness & Discovery

These are the channels that bring new customers into the Louzan universe for the first time.

**TikTok Content Engine**
Post 3–5 videos per week on @louzanfashion — abaya styling, factory behind-the-scenes, new arrivals, fragrance unboxings. All in Arabic. The TikTok Pixel is already installed; the content just needs to start. Partner with 2–3 UAE-based modest fashion micro-influencers to amplify reach. This is the highest-growth opportunity available right now, and competitors like Kamin are already building this audience.

**Arabic SEO**
Translate the full website into Arabic with proper Arabic meta tags, headings, and URL slugs. Every major competitor has already done this. Louzan is currently invisible to Arabic-language searches — the single largest addressable audience for abaya keywords in the UAE. Fixing this alone could double organic search visibility within 6 months.

**Snapchat Activation**
Louzan already has 71–76K Snapchat followers — a dormant asset. Snapchat remains disproportionately strong in the GCC. Publishing a consistent Stories cadence (new arrivals, styling tips, seasonal campaigns) costs nothing additional and activates a built-in audience.

**Seasonal Landing Pages**
Build a dedicated Ramadan/Eid landing page, indexed well in advance of the season. The highest-traffic period for abaya brands deserves a proper campaign home — not just a product grid.

---

## Mid Funnel — Convert Interest Into Intent

These are the levers that take someone who's discovered Louzan and move them toward a purchase.

**Instagram Shopping**
With 146K followers, Instagram is Louzan's strongest platform — but it drives zero direct revenue without Shopping tags. Activating the Shopify–Instagram connection adds a direct "buy now" pathway to every post and Story. Takes less than a week to set up.

**WhatsApp Business**
Add a WhatsApp chat button to the website and enable automated product enquiry flows. In the UAE, WhatsApp is how customers ask questions before buying. A quick response on WhatsApp converts at 3–5x the rate of email. Essential for both B2C sales and the B2B/franchise pipeline.

**Reviews on Product Pages**
Move the reviews widget to immediately below the product title and price. This single change typically lifts fashion ecommerce conversion rates by 25–30%. The Stamped.io system is already installed — this is a theme edit, not a new build.

**Abaya Styling Quiz**
A 5-question "Find Your Perfect Abaya" quiz that ends with personalised product recommendations and an opt-in for WhatsApp or email. Reduces decision fatigue, increases confidence, and captures customer data for future marketing.

---

## Bottom Funnel — Why This All Matters

When the top and mid funnel are working, the bottom funnel — purchase, repeat purchase, loyalty — compounds. Customers who discover Louzan through TikTok, trust it through reviews and WhatsApp, and can buy directly through Instagram Shopping or the website with minimal friction will convert at meaningfully higher rates. Over time, a loyalty programme, abandoned cart automation, and post-purchase sequences lock in repeat buyers and increase lifetime value. The GCC abaya market is growing at 12% CAGR with 68% of purchases now made digitally — the brands that build digital loyalty now will own the next decade.
